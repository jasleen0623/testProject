For my project I have decided to create a website called Edu-Unite. 
In this application there will be lectures uploaded by the professionals, 
teachers and even the students. 
With this application students can get answers to the questions they are 
struggling with. They can even upload the question's photo rather than typing it.


This application will even keep a record of the progress, 
marks and other activities of the students and suggest them 
with the learning methods.
